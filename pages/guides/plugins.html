Plugins and Addons

Newcoming uses a range of vanilla-friendly plugins and addons to add to your roleplay experience. You can familiarize yourself with our list of modifications and their functionality in here or discover things in-game, that's up to you!

No Name Tags

To encourage RP and discourage metagaming, all name tags are invisible while a player is in character. This is mainly to prevent people from seeing name tags through walls.

Claims

Invisible magic protects your home and assets against harm! Be careful not to leave your door open though, because CHESTS, BARRELS AND OTHER CONTAINERS are PUBLICLY ACCESSIBLE. Make use of redstone mechanisms and obscure storage rooms to protect your belongings. You are able to earn additional claim blocks through playtime or by donating to the server.

RP Chat/Voice Chat

Use /ic and /ooc to switch between local in character chat and global out of character chat. You can also use /whisper to talk to people in character in a very small radius. We also use the Simple Voice Chat mod to allow players to talk to each other using microphones! There are 2 ranges of the voice chat, the default one, and a whisper option which can only be heard in a small radius.

Downing Plugin

Upon being "killed," the dead player enters a downed state where they can be searched, picked up and carried, revived, or killed. As the victim, when downed you are set to half health, unable to break or place blocks, unable to eat or regenerate health, and unable to fight. You can yell for help, beg for mercy, or accept your fate by holding SHIFT - this will instantly kill you. Be warned when choosing death during a lore situation: you will be eligible for LORE DEATH based on your choice. As an onlooker, you can save a downed player by holding SHIFT near them and letting the revive timer fill up. Alternatively, right click to pick them up or SHIFT + right click to access their inventory. Be warned, downed players are canonically considered incapacitated, not unconscious; your actions will have consequences.

Combat Tag

Got attacked? Don't log out! Combat tag is designed to prevent unfair and unrealistic escapes from fights. Logging out while combat tagged results in a fake player spawning in your stead - an immobile and defenseless avatar that your pursuers are free to kill. While combat tagged you also lose access to commands like /tpa, land claiming and more.

OOC Mode

Building while listening to lo-fi tunes? Not feeling like interacting while grinding copious amounts of resources? Run the command /glow gui : this will let you pick a color to glow as to clearly indicate to others that you are OOC. Any interactions while glowing like this are considered OOC, even if someone that isn't glowing interacts with you. Just make sure you pick a color other than white, as white is considered IC! Make sure not to use this command to escape plot ramifications or to leave an ongoing lore situation; such behaviors can result in a ban based on severity.

Seasons

To reflect the passage of time, seasons change in-game! While mostly cosmetic, you might find your farms not as bountiful during winter season and you might see unexpected ambient mobs spawning near your dwelling depending on the time of year. Explore and enjoy!

Brewing

Brew spirits and drinks with peculiar effects! Use a cauldron with a campfire underneath and experiment with ingredients.

Nether and End Materials

The Nether and End are inaccessible but we recognize the riches of those dimensions are valued for many reasons. As such, most resources are accessible either through crafting or through custom trades with merchants at the spawn marketplace. Limited amounts of Netherite are brought into this world through meteor showers.

Dice Roll

Let luck have a hand in your fate! Roll a dice with the command /roll 20 (or any other number of your choosing) and have the result displayed in local chat. Players are encouraged to use Dice Rolls in roleplay, for example, to determine their success in a game of poker. Dice Rolls are also used by staff to decide the success of heists, prison breaks and assassinations. For more information please consult The Rules Page

Custom Warps

Tired of traveling around the continent by foot? You can set up ships, trains, and other forms of transportation to get a Custom Warp established! If you have 8 Netherite blocks, you can even make your own portals!

Bottle EXP

Punch an enchantment table with a glass bottle to store your EXP! Note: Typically, “Bottles O’ Enchanting” have a randomized amount of EXP, 3-11 points. Our server has a set value of 7 to make storing and using XP more reliable.

Crop EXP

Harvesting most kinds of crops will give you EXP!

Custom Recipes and Items

We have quite a few custom recipes on our server! This includes custom foods for players to discover, as well as quality of life additions to vanilla, like being able to use the stone cutter on wood AND being able to grind down stone into cobblestone, cobblestone into sand, and sand into gravel with the stone cutter! You may also be lucky enough to come across a rare "relic," an item with unique properties…

Custom Fishing

There’s a lot more types of fish in Penora to reel in! Some have unique uses as well.

Custom Images

Use the /imageframe command to make your own custom map art! You can also use shears on item frames to make them invisible!

Mines

Iron and gold farms aren’t allowed on our server, but there are other ways to automate getting ore! Talk to staff about setting up a mine! Mines work 24/7 and can generate other kinds of ore than just iron and gold. The location and quality of the mine are what goes into determining what they can output and how fast they do so. Note: We may be open to the idea of passive generation of other kinds of items if you have any ideas!

Custom Crops

Penoran plant life is quite a bit more diverse than vanilla Minecraft! You can find and purchase more kinds of crops like rice, grapes, and corn! Each of these crops will have their own unique recipes associated with them; some of them can even be brewed!

TPA

Players can use the /tpa command to teleport to each other both in AND out of character. However, this ability uses 20 levels of EXP. Also, if you are a part of a nation that has a National Anchor, you can warp to that Anchor for 20 levels.
