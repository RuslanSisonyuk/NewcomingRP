Nimue Argorim
A curiously lost elf.

Nimue Argorim was born a Qu'Essan, or Princess, of House Baenre, a noble house of the Dark elves. She was not fully raised in the Underdark, but she was taught the ways of her people, or half her people... She was born Half Moon elf, and Half Drow. Nimue was forced to do horrible things by her father- most of which consisted of killing the people she cared about. One day- her sister decided that enough was enough- and helped Nimue escape the realm that she was born into. Arriving in Penora, Nimue was lost and confused- but she was free. But now... months after arriving in Penora- something has changed, more importantly, Nimue had changed. She was no longer a Drow, or a Moon elf- she was something else entirely. A Faelari Gray Elf.
