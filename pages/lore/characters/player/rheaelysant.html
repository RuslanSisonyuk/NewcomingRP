Rhea Elysant
A lone Ademora

Rhea Elysant, born Astraea del Ademora, is a curious individual that was brough into this world on Winter 21 over a thousand years ago. She grew up with her family and the community of her people. One day, a raid occured on her home, masked men burned down the forest that she and her people resided in- and took a plethora of them as hostages. At the time Rhea had white hair and silvery eyes, so the people that had captured her, and the ones that sold her, believed they could convince a kindom that she was an angel of sorts. Was she an angel? No.
