Penora
The Gods of the World

There are a plethora of gods that reside within the realm of Penora. Most are known by the people of this land- but may be more than what meets they eye to these deities. Most gods have followers, and some even have Priests/Priestesses!

Orella

The Goddess of Nature and Motherhood. Typically referred to as Mother or Mother Orella.

Garuuni

The god of the Dwarves and the Craft. Typically referred to as the Stone father by the Dwarves.

Y'Garosh

The God of Water and Wisdom. This God is split into multiple beings currently.

Nyssenius

The Goddess of Death. Not much is known about this Goddess... yet.

Tha'ulla

The goddess of War and Fair Duels. Can get pretty angry when someone dishonors a duel.
