The Ademora People.
A winged race- that lived a long time ago...

A peaceful race that once lived in the same realm of Penora... Not of this continent, but of another. These people tend to stay within their forests, and are known for their long lifespans. They have wings that are attached to their back- similar to that of a bird. The Ademora were hunted long ago for their wings, and their numbers have dwindled since then. They had a strong connection to nature- and tended to forests, caring for them as if they were a part of their clans. Currently the Only known Ademora is Rhea Elysant, and even then she no longer has her wings that signify her heritage.
